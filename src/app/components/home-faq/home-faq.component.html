Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

What's the difference between CactusSoft and CactusSoft+?

CactusSoft+ includes all modules from the basic subscription, plus a few more (AI etc.)

What's CactusSoft+ price?

The initial cost is set by NFT sellers on OpenSea. Renewal is 0.025 ETH/month for a Green Cactus and is free for a Gold one.

Can I resell my license?

All unbindable CactusSoft licenses are resellable, including CactusSoft+ licenses (you can resell it via OpenSea).

Can I get a refund?

Unfortunately, all sales are final,
and no refunds will be processed.
